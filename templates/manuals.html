Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to use the system:

  • Step 1: Visit the upload page and select your PDF document.
  • Step 2: Input one or multiple genetic variants following the required format.
  • Step 3: Review the classification results, which include detailed risk evaluations and possible treatment implications.

For further assistance, check out our User Documentation.
